Payment

We have methods that analyze the internal consistency of your answers. We also analyze the consistency between your answer and the answers of your fellow workers. In addition, we also check your answers against references we know to be accurate. We will use these methods to rank the submitted assignments according to their quality.


For this experiment we will pay a base reward of $0.10/HIT for every accepted HIT. We have made available a set of 30 different HITs. You will receive a bonus of:
  • $0.02/HIT (for a total of $0.12/HIT) if you submit more than 20 HITs or
  • $0.05/HIT (for a total of $0.15/HIT) if you submit more than 20 HITs and your results are among the top 20%
Bonuses will be paid up to 7 days after submission, because we can only rank the submissions once we have a significant number of answers. The base reward will always be paid within 24 hours of submission.
